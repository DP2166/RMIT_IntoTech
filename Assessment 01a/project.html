Project Idea

Overview

One IT project idea I have is to create a 'screen time' application where you can see your personal or even your kids screen time from your mobile phone to laptop and computer. If you download the application on those devices, you will be able to read the information that is transformed into easier readable information, in the form of visual data such as graphs and numbers. People would have to create accounts so that their data is saved, and this is how all devices are able to be linked together as it is under one account. One other important feature is that small family groups would be arranges so that if parents would want to monitor their child's screen time, then they are able to see the total amount of screen time they experience week/day (depends on the type of sorting you want).

Motivation

In the 21st century more technology is improving and are becoming essential in our everyday lives but too much screen time is not health as study shown it would "make it hard for your child to sleep at night" and "raise your child's risk for attention problems, anxiety, and depression". So, by developing this application people would be able to visually see their hours spend on their screens which would motivate them to decrease their usage of screen devices allowing people to spend on other needs. The main motivation behind this IT project is to make people realise the number of hours people spend on their screens every day. This is because majority of people think of mobile phone screen time and skip out on the screen time of spend on PCs, laptops, TVs, and consoles which could be a significant amount.

I believe this IT project idea is important as it would allow everyday people to easily tell how much hours are spent on screen time. So, by coming up with an application that can be downloaded on multiple devices it could determine peoples actual screen time by displaying graphs that is colour coded to sort out which devices is which and as well as time spend on those certain apps. Within the app there would be a family group where clients are able to monitor those who is a part of the family pack.
